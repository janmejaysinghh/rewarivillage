About Rewari ( रेवाड़ी ) Village

Rewari is a Village in Jalalpur Block in Saran District of Bihar State, India. It belongs to Saran Division

Rewari is the village of hindu religious. Total 23 caste lives in this village. Chhath pooja is one of the best festival to see the brotherhood of the villagers. People of this village always support each other.

Population

Rewari village has a total population according to the 2011 census about 7500 residents.

Distances

Distance from the District head quarters 5 km From Chhapra

Distance from the State Capital 59 km From Patna

Nearest Hospital: 10 km (Sadar Hospital Saran)

Nearest Railway Station: 10 km (Chhapra Railway Station)

Nearest Airport: 75 km (Jai Prakash Narayan Airport, Patna)
